## How to Upgrade Your Plan

### Option 1: Self-Service (Instant)
1. Open TeleNova App → Account → Plan & Services
2. Select "Change Plan"
3. Choose new plan; review pro-rated charges
4. Confirm upgrade — effective immediately

### Option 2: Online Portal
1. Visit account.telenovaconnect.com
2. Navigate to Plan Management
3. Select desired plan and confirm

### Option 3: Support Chat or Phone
- Chat with an agent to get personalized recommendations
- Upgrades processed within 15 minutes

---

## Upgrade Policies

- **Upgrades**: Effective immediately; pro-rated credit for remaining days on old plan
- **Downgrades**: Take effect at the next billing cycle
- **Cancellations**: 30-day notice required; early termination not applicable (all plans are month-to-month)
- **Plan lock-in**: No annual contracts — all plans are month-to-month
- **Promotional offers**: Existing customers can contact loyalty team for matching new-customer promotions

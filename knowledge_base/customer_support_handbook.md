# Customer Support Handbook - TeleNova

## Our Support Promise

TeleNova commits to: fast resolution, honest communication, and treating every customer with respect. Every support interaction should leave the customer feeling heard, helped, and valued.

## Account Information & Verification

### Verifying Customer Identity
Before making any account changes, verify identity using at least 2 of the following:
1. Account number
2. Last 4 digits of SSN/Tax ID (business accounts)
3. PIN / Passcode set at account creation
4. Last payment amount
5. Billing address ZIP code
6. Device IMEI

**High-risk actions** (plan changes, port-out, address changes) require all 3 of: account number + PIN + one additional factor.

### Account Status Types
- **Active**: Full service, in good standing
- **Suspended (voluntary)**: Customer-requested suspension; $10/month to hold number
- **Suspended (non-payment)**: Service interrupted; incoming calls allowed; balance due shown
- **Restricted**: Under fraud review; no account changes permitted pending investigation
- **Closed**: Account terminated; number released after 90 days

## Common Account Requests

### How to Check Account Balance
- **App**: Home screen shows current balance and due date
- **Text**: Send BAL to 8765
- **IVR**: Call 1-800-TELNOVA; say "account balance"

### How to Request a Payment Extension
- Available once per 6-month period
- Extends due date by 7 days
- Must be requested before the due date
- Available via app, portal, or support chat

### How to Add/Remove a Line
- Adding a line: Available via app, portal, or support call; instant activation
- Removing a line: 30-day notice required; pro-rated charges for current billing cycle
- Porting a number in: Provide carrier account number + PIN from previous carrier; process takes 2–4 hours
- Porting out: Provide TeleNova account number + PIN to new carrier; TeleNova cannot block a port-out request

### How to Unlock a Device
- **Paid-in-full devices**: Instant unlock via app or portal
- **Installment devices**: Must complete installments or pay remaining balance; 2-business-day processing
- **International unlock**: Available for eligible devices after 60 days of active service

## Ticket Management

### Ticket Types
- **Billing Dispute** (BIL-XXXXX): Payment and charge issues
- **Network Issue** (NET-XXXXX): Connectivity and signal problems
- **Account** (ACC-XXXXX): Account modifications and inquiries
- **Device** (DEV-XXXXX): Hardware and device issues
- **General** (GEN-XXXXX): Miscellaneous inquiries

### Ticket Statuses
- **Open**: Ticket logged; assigned to appropriate team
- **In Progress**: Agent or team actively working on the issue
- **Pending Customer**: Awaiting additional information from customer
- **Resolved**: Issue resolved; solution documented
- **Closed**: Customer confirmed resolution; ticket archived

### Checking Ticket Status
- App: Support → My Tickets
- Online: account.telenovaconnect.com → Support History
- Text: Send TICKET [ticket number] to 8765
- Chat/Phone: Provide ticket number to agent

### Estimated Resolution Times
- Billing disputes: 3–5 business days
- Network issues: 24–72 hours
- Account changes: Same session or 1 business day
- Device issues: 1–3 business days (warranty claims up to 7 days)

## Service Guarantee & Compensation Policy

### TeleNova Service Guarantee
- Network uptime SLA: 99.9% monthly
- If SLA not met, prorated bill credit automatically applied
- Bill credit formula: (Outage hours / 720) × Monthly plan cost

### Customer Goodwill Credits
Support agents are authorized to apply the following credits without manager approval:
- First billing error: Full credit of incorrect charge
- Network outage > 4 hours: 1 day service credit
- Customer inconvenience (prolonged support interaction): Up to $20 credit
- Loyalty acknowledgment: Up to $15 credit for accounts > 3 years

Credits exceeding $50 require supervisor approval.

## Frequently Asked Questions by Customers

### "Can I keep my phone number if I switch plans?"
Yes. Your number is tied to your account, not your plan. Switching plans within TeleNova never affects your number.

### "What happens to my data if I switch to a lower plan mid-cycle?"
Your current cycle runs to completion on the old plan. The new plan starts at the next billing cycle. You won't lose data from the current cycle.

### "Can I pause my service while traveling?"
Yes. Voluntary suspension is available for 30–90 days. Your number is held, and you're charged $10/month during suspension. Autopay is paused during this period.

### "Is there a senior or military discount?"
- Senior discount (65+): $10 off per line per month on Starter or Connect plans (verified via government ID)
- Military/veteran discount: 15% off total monthly bill (verified via ID.me)
- First responder discount: 15% off total monthly bill (verified via ID.me)

### "My device was stolen. What should I do?"
1. Report to local law enforcement and get a case number
2. Contact TeleNova immediately to suspend service and blacklist the IMEI
3. File a claim if you have device protection insurance
4. Purchase or finance a replacement device
5. Your number and account remain active during suspension

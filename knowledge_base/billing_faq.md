# Billing FAQ - TeleNova Customer Support

## General Billing Questions

### When is my bill due?
Your bill is due on the same date each month — the date you activated your account. For example, if you activated on the 15th, your bill is due on the 15th of every month. Bills are generated 7 days before the due date. Grace period of 5 days applies before service interruption.

### How can I view my bill?
You can view your bill through:
- **My TeleNova App** (iOS and Android): Tap "Account" → "Billing & Payments"
- **Online Portal**: Visit account.telenovaconnect.com
- **SMS**: Text BILL to 8765
- **Automated IVR**: Call 1-800-TELNOVA and say "billing"

### Why is my bill higher than usual?
Common reasons for a higher-than-expected bill:
1. **International roaming charges**: If you traveled abroad and used data or calls, roaming rates apply. Check your recent travel dates.
2. **Overage charges**: Exceeding your plan's data, talk, or text limits triggers per-unit overage fees.
3. **One-time fees**: Device installment charges, plan change fees, or late payment fees.
4. **Pro-rated charges**: Mid-cycle plan upgrades result in a pro-rated charge for the remainder of the billing period.
5. **Premium services**: Subscriptions to add-ons like international calling, streaming bundles, or device protection.

### What are the overage charges?
- **Data overage**: $10 per GB beyond plan limit (capped at $50/month overage maximum)
- **Talk overage** (limited-minute plans only): $0.10 per minute
- **Text overage** (limited-text plans only): $0.05 per message
- **International SMS**: $0.25 per message sent

### How do I dispute a charge?
To dispute a charge:
1. Contact support within **60 days** of the charge appearing on your bill
2. Provide the charge date, amount, and reason for dispute
3. Our billing team investigates within **3–5 business days**
4. If valid, credits are applied to the next billing cycle
5. Disputes can be filed via app, online portal, phone, or chat

### Can I set up AutoPay?
Yes. AutoPay discounts $5/month off your bill. Accepted payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit cards
- Bank account (ACH transfer)
- UPI / Digital wallets (in select regions)

AutoPay processes 1 day before your due date. You'll receive a reminder 3 days prior.

### What happens if I miss a payment?
- **Day 1–5 past due**: Grace period, no interruption
- **Day 6**: $10 late fee applied, email/SMS reminder sent
- **Day 15 past due**: Service may be suspended (incoming calls still possible)
- **Day 30 past due**: Account may be sent to collections; device unlock may be restricted
- **Restoring service**: Pay the outstanding balance + $25 reconnection fee

### Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees. All fees are disclosed at purchase. Standard fees include:
- **Regulatory Recovery Fee**: $3.50/line/month (covers spectrum licenses, E911 infrastructure)
- **Federal Universal Service Fund (USF)**: ~5.4% of interstate charges
- **State and local taxes**: Vary by location (typically 8–15%)
- **Paper bill fee**: $2.50/month (waived with e-billing enrollment)

## Payment Methods

### Can I pay in installments?
For outstanding balances over $100, TeleNova offers payment plans:
- 3-month plan: No interest
- 6-month plan: 0% interest for account holders in good standing
- Contact billing to enroll

### How do I update my payment method?
- App: Account → Payment Methods → Add/Edit
- Online: account.telenovaconnect.com → Billing → Payment Settings
- Call 1-800-TELNOVA: Automated system accepts credit/debit card updates

# Escalation Standard Operating Procedure - TeleNova

## Escalation Philosophy

TeleNova's escalation process ensures customers receive resolution at the appropriate level without unnecessary transfers. Every escalation must be documented, warm-handed, and followed up within the committed timeframe.

---

## Escalation Tiers

### Tier 1: AI Assistant / Self-Service
**Handles**: Billing queries, plan questions, basic troubleshooting, account information
**SLA**: Immediate response; resolution within same session
**Escalation trigger**: Unable to resolve within 3 turns on same topic

### Tier 2: Live Agent (Chat / Phone)
**Handles**: Complex billing disputes, SIM replacements, account verification, complaints
**SLA**: Wait time ≤ 5 minutes (chat), ≤ 10 minutes (phone)
**Hours**: 24/7
**Escalation trigger**: Customer request, unresolved T1 issue, account security concern

### Tier 3: Specialist Team
**Handles**: Legal disputes, fraud investigation, network engineering issues, executive complaints, account retention
**SLA**: 24-hour callback commitment; resolution within 3 business days
**Hours**: Monday–Friday 8 AM – 8 PM local time
**Escalation trigger**: T2 agent unable to resolve, billing dispute > $200, fraud flag, churn risk

### Tier 4: Executive Resolution Team
**Handles**: Regulatory complaints, media/press escalations, executive contacts
**SLA**: Same-day acknowledgment; resolution within 5 business days
**Trigger**: Social media viral risk, regulatory body involvement, C-suite contact

## Escalation Triggers by Category

### Billing Escalations
- Dispute amount exceeds $100
- Customer has been charged incorrectly for 2+ billing cycles
- Fraudulent account activity suspected
- Payment arrangement needed > $500

### Network Escalations
- Issue persists after full troubleshooting (all steps exhausted)
- Multiple customers in same area affected
- Service outage exceeding 4 hours
- Infrastructure damage reported

### Account Escalations
- Account compromised / unauthorized changes
- Identity verification failed (cannot verify customer)
- Account restriction due to suspected fraud
- Porting issues (customer switching carriers)

### Customer Experience Escalations
- Customer expresses extreme dissatisfaction (3+ sessions, same issue)
- Customer requests supervisor or manager
- Customer mentions regulatory body or legal action
- Customer is a high-value account (Unlimited Pro or Business Pro with 5+ lines)

## Escalation Handoff Protocol

### AI-to-Human Handoff
1. Summarize the conversation context (intent, issue, steps taken, customer sentiment)
2. Collect customer's callback number if escalating to phone
3. Inform customer of expected wait time
4. Transfer context packet to receiving agent:
   - Session ID
   - Issue category
   - Troubleshooting steps already attempted
   - Customer tone/sentiment assessment
   - Account tier

### Warm Transfer Protocol (Agent-to-Agent)
1. Receiving agent introduced before transferring customer
2. Brief the receiving agent with customer name, account number, and issue summary
3. Customer should never have to repeat the full issue
4. Transferring agent remains on hold for 60 seconds to ensure smooth handoff

## Escalation Response Templates

### Escalating to Live Agent (AI Handoff Script)
> "I understand this is a frustrating situation, and I want to make sure you get the right help. I'm connecting you with a TeleNova specialist who will have our full conversation history and can resolve this for you directly. Your wait time is approximately [X] minutes. Is there anything else I should pass along to them?"

### Escalation to T3 Specialist
> "I've escalated your case to our specialist team. You'll receive a callback within 24 hours at [phone number on file]. Your case reference number is [CASE-XXXXX]. Is this the best number to reach you?"

### Acknowledging Customer Frustration
> "I completely understand why this is frustrating — this shouldn't have happened, and you deserve a quick resolution. I'm making sure this is treated as a priority."

## SLA Commitments

| Tier | First Response | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| T1 (AI) | Immediate | Same session |
| T2 (Agent) | < 5 min chat, < 10 min phone | Same session or 24 hrs |
| T3 (Specialist) | 24-hour callback | 3 business days |
| T4 (Executive) | Same-day acknowledgment | 5 business days |

---

## Post-Escalation Follow-Up

- T2 cases: Automated satisfaction survey sent 2 hours after resolution
- T3 cases: Agent follow-up call within 24 hours of resolution
- T4 cases: Account manager follow-up; goodwill gesture authorized (bill credit up to $50)

---

## Prohibited Escalation Behaviors

- Never place a customer on hold for more than 3 minutes without checking in
- Never transfer a customer more than twice without supervisor authorization
- Never close a case without confirming the customer's issue is resolved
- Never promise a resolution timeframe that cannot be guaranteed
